This game is an online locally played tic-tac-toe game. That's right, we are taking you back to the 1990's where everyone had to share a screen!

To play, click on a space on the tic-tac-toe board to claim it. Once a player has 3 spots filled vertically, horizontally, or diagonally, the player wins.
