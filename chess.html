This game is an online local player chess game. All of the pieces are arranged neatly at the bottom.

To start the game, begin by dragging the pieces with your mouse and dropping them into place on the board.

There are no rules and as a result, you can place pieces where ever you want. This will partly be a game of trust :)

Note: It is pretty easy to get the queen and king mixed up. Remember: The Queen always goes on her own color.
